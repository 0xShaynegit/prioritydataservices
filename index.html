The whole process

No mystery. Here's exactly what happens.

  1. A

    The chat

    30 minutes, free, no obligation. Phone, video or coffee in Hobart. We learn your situation and goals.

  2. B

    The shortlist

    We compare the panel and present 2 to 3 genuine options with the trade-offs spelled out in plain English.

  3. C

    The paperwork

    We prepare and lodge the application, chase the lender, and keep you updated at every stage. You sign, we sweat.

    The keys

    Settlement day. Then we review your loan every year, because the best rate today won't be the best rate forever.
